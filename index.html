Biography


I’m a researcher in Healthcare Data Analytics, focusing on the application of machine learning and optimization to address critical challenges in healthcare. My research explores data-driven modeling of cognitive aging, HIV, and SARS-CoV-2, aiming to advance early detection, personalized interventions, and systems-level understanding of health and disease dynamics.
